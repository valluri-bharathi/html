Aloo fry

Ingredients

  • oil
  • potato
  • chilli powder
  • salt

Steps For Preparation

  1. light the gas
  2. put a vessel
  3. add oil
  4. add cut potatoes
  5. put salt
  6. put chilli powder
  7. fry the potatoes till crispy texture
  8. aloo fry is ready to serve
